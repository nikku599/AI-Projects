Strict laws for LLM models risk stifling innovation and delaying technological progress that can benefit society. Overregulation may create barriers for researchers and developers, hindering advancements in education, healthcare, and accessibility. Additionally, overly rigid legal frameworks can become obsolete quickly in the fast-evolving AI landscape, preventing adaptive responses to new challenges. Instead of strict laws, we should promote responsible development through voluntary standards and industry best practices that balance innovation with safety, ensuring that AI advancements are guided by ethical principles without unnecessarily constraining beneficial progress.
